MOOSE I

MOOSE I - an overview of the program and initial fits to high-state spectra during the 2021 excursion compared to high-state spectra taken by XMM-Newton in 2016. We found some evidence for an increase in the line-of-sight absorption, but the intrinsic spectral properties did not vary significantly during the excursion.
